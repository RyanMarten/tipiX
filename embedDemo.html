tipiX: embedding demo



You can now embed tipiX datasets in other webpages.
On this page we demonstrate some new features of tipiX and embedding tipiXes.

Two-dimentional Exploration


Here is an example of standard tipiX behaviour: A dataset that can be explored in two directions - time of day (top to bottom) and different days (left to right).
Just move the mouse around to explore the space.


One-dimentional Exploration (e.g. Timelapse)

For timelapses, we can start them in play mode, with the "&play=true" address bar option.
To stop a timelapse from playing, simply click on the timelapse, which starts the exploration mode (move the mouse left to right to visualize the frames). To freeze a frame, press x while stopped.
